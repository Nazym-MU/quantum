## Key Formulas
$$\langle +b|+a\rangle = \cos\frac{\theta_{ab}}{2}$$
$$P(+a;+b) = \frac{1}{2}\sin^2\frac{\theta_{ab}}{2}$$
$$P(+a;+b) \leq P(+a;+c) + P(+c;+b) \quad \text{(Bell's Inequality)}$$
