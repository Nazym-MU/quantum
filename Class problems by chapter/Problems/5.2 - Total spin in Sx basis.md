Express the total-spin $s = 1$ states of two spin-$\frac{1}{2}$ particles given in (5.30a) and (5.30c) in terms of the states $\ket{+x, +x}$, $\ket{+x, -x}$, $\ket{-x, +x}$, and $\ket{-x, -x}$.

---
## Solution
We need to express $\ket{1,1} = \ket{+z,+z}$ in terms of $\ket{+x,+x}$, $\ket{+x,-x}$, $\ket{-x,+x}$, $\ket{-x,-x}$.
For a single spin-$\frac{1}{2}$ particle:
$$\ket{+x} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\ket{+z} + \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\ket{-z} \qquad \ket{-x} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\ket{+z} - \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\ket{-z}$$
$$\ket{+z} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\ket{+x} + \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\ket{-x} \qquad \ket{-z} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\ket{+x} - \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\ket{-x}$$
### Express $\ket{1,1} = \ket{+z,+z}$ in the $x$-basis:
$$\ket{1,1} = \ket{+z,+z} = \ket{+z}_1 \otimes \ket{+z}_2$$
$$= \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\ket{+x}_1 + \ket{-x}_1\right) \otimes \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\ket{+x}_2 + \ket{-x}_2\right)$$
$$= \frac{1}{2}\left(\ket{+x,+x} - \ket{+x,-x} + \ket{-x,+x} - \ket{-x,-x}\right)$$
$$\boxed{\ket{1,1} = \frac{1}{2}\left(\ket{+x,+x} - \ket{+x,-x} + \ket{-x,+x} - \ket{-x,-x}\right)}$$
