Take the spin Hamiltonian for the hydrogen atom in an external magnetic field $B_0$ in the $z$ direction to be
$$\hat{H} = \frac{2A}{\hbar^2}\hat{\mathbf{S}}_1 \cdot \hat{\mathbf{S}}_2 + \omega_0 \hat{S}_{1z}$$
where $\omega_0 = \frac{geB_0}{2mc}$, with $m$ the mass of the electron. The contribution $-\hat{\boldsymbol{\mu}}_2 \cdot \mathbf{B}_0$ of the proton has been neglected because the mass of the proton is roughly 2000 times larger than the mass of the electron. Determine the energies of this system.

---
## Solution
$$2\hat{\mathbf{S}}_1 \cdot \hat{\mathbf{S}}_2 = 2\hat{S}_{1x}\hat{S}_{2x} + 2\hat{S}_{1y}\hat{S}_{2y} + 2\hat{S}_{1z}\hat{S}_{2z}$$
$$= \hat{S}_{1+}\hat{S}_{2-} + \hat{S}_{1-}\hat{S}_{2+} + 2\hat{S}_{1z}\hat{S}_{2z}$$
### 4Ã—4 Matrix Representation
$$\langle 1|\hat{H}|1\rangle = \frac{A}{\hbar^2}\bra{+z, +z}(\hat{S}_{1+}\hat{S}_{2-} + \hat{S}_{1-}\hat{S}_{2+} + 2\hat{S}_{1z}\hat{S}_{2z})\ket{+z, +z} + \omega_0\bra{+z, +z}\hat{S}_{1z}\ket{+z, +z}=$$
$$= \frac{A}{\hbar^2} \cdot \frac{\hbar^2}{2} + \omega_0 \cdot \frac{\hbar}{2} = \frac{A + \omega_0\hbar}{2}$$
Similarly, 
$$\langle 2|\hat{H}|2\rangle = \frac{-A + \omega_0\hbar}{2}$$

$$\langle 3|\hat{H}|3\rangle = \frac{-A - \omega_0\hbar}{2}$$
$$\langle 4|\hat{H}|4\rangle = \frac{A - \omega_0\hbar}{2}$$
### Non-zero Off-Diagonal Elements
$$\langle 3|\hat{H}|2\rangle = \frac{A}{\hbar^2}\langle -\tfrac{1}{2},+\tfrac{1}{2}|(\hat{S}_{1+}\hat{S}_{2-} + \hat{S}_{1-}\hat{S}_{2+} + 2\hat{S}_{1z}\hat{S}_{2z})|+\tfrac{1}{2},-\tfrac{1}{2}\rangle + \omega_0\langle -\tfrac{1}{2},+\tfrac{1}{2}|\hat{S}_{1z}|+\tfrac{1}{2},-\tfrac{1}{2}\rangle = A$$
Similarly, $\langle 2|\hat{H}|3\rangle = A$
### Matrix

$$\begin{pmatrix}
\frac{A+\hbar\omega_0}{2} & 0 & 0 & 0\\
0 & \frac{-A+\hbar\omega_0}{2} & A & 0\\
0 & A & \frac{-A-\hbar\omega_0}{2} & 0\\
0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{A-\hbar\omega_0}{2}
\end{pmatrix}
\begin{pmatrix}
\langle 1|\psi\rangle\\
\langle 2|\psi\rangle\\
\langle 3|\psi\rangle\\
\langle 4|\psi\rangle
\end{pmatrix}
= E
\begin{pmatrix}
\langle 1|\psi\rangle\\
\langle 2|\psi\rangle\\
\langle 3|\psi\rangle\\
\langle 4|\psi\rangle
\end{pmatrix}$$
### Eigenvalues
Setting the determinant equal to zero:
$$\begin{vmatrix}
\frac{A+\hbar\omega_0}{2} - E & 0 & 0 & 0\\
0 & \frac{-A+\hbar\omega_0}{2} - E & A & 0\\
0 & A & \frac{-A-\hbar\omega_0}{2} - E & 0\\
0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{A-\hbar\omega_0}{2} - E
\end{vmatrix} = 0$$
First row, first column and last row, last column are already eigenvalues:
$$E_1 = \frac{A+\hbar\omega_0}{2}, \quad E_4 = \frac{A-\hbar\omega_0}{2}$$
Then we evaluate the middle block:

$$\left(\frac{-A+\hbar\omega_0}{2} - E\right)\left(\frac{-A-\hbar\omega_0}{2} - E\right) - A^2 = 0$$

$$E^2 + AE - \frac{\hbar^2\omega_0^2}{4} = 0$$
