The column vector representing the state $\ket{\psi}$ is given by
$$\ket\psi\xrightarrow[S_z \text{ basis}]{}\frac{1}{\sqrt3}\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ \sqrt2\end{pmatrix}$$
Use matrix mechanics to determine $\braket{S_x}$ for this state.

---
### Solution
From [[2.10 - Change of Basis + Transformation]], we know $S_x \to \frac{\hbar}{2}\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$ in the $S_z$ basis.
Expectation value:
$$\braket{S_x}=\braket{\psi|S_x|\psi}=\frac{\hbar}{6}\begin{bmatrix}1 & \sqrt{2} \end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ \sqrt{2} \end{bmatrix} = \frac{\sqrt2}{3}\hbar$$
