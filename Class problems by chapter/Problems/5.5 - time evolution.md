At time $t = 0$, an electron and a positron are formed in a state with total spin angular momentum equal to zero, perhaps from the decay of a spinless particle. The particles are situated in a uniform magnetic field $B_0$ in the $z$ direction.
Interaction between the electron and the positron may be neglected, so the spin Hamiltonian of the system may be written as $\hat{H} = \omega_0(\hat{S}_{1z} - \hat{S}_{2z})$, where $\hat{S}_1$ is the spin operator of the electron, $\hat{S}_2$ is the spin operator of the positron, and $\omega_0$ is a constant.

(b) What is the spin state of the system at time $t$? Show that the state of the system oscillates between a spin-0 and a spin-1 state. Determine the period of oscillation.

(c) At time $t$, measurements are made of $S_{1x}$ and $S_{2x}$. Calculate the probability that *both* of these measurements yield the value $\hbar/2$.

---
### Solution
### Part (b)
**Initial state:** Because $\hat{H}$ involves $z$-component of the spin, we express it in the $S_z$ basis:
$$\ket{0,0} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\ket{+z,-z} - \ket{-z,+z}\right)$$
**Time evolution:** 
$$\ket{\psi(t)} = e^{-i\hat{H}t/\hbar}\ket{\psi(0)}$$
**Computing eigenvalues:**
$$\hat{H}\ket{+z,-z} = \omega_0(\hat{S}_{1z} - \hat{S}_{2z})\ket{+z,-z} = \omega_0\left(\frac{\hbar}{2} - \left(-\frac{\hbar}{2}\right)\right)\ket{+z,-z} = \omega_0\hbar\ket{+z,-z}$$
$$\hat{H}\ket{-z,+z} = \omega_0(\hat{S}_{1z} - \hat{S}_{2z})\ket{-z,+z} = -\omega_0\hbar\ket{-z,+z}$$
Eigenstates, so just evolve by a phase factor:
$$\ket{\psi(t)} = e^{-i\hat{H}t/\hbar}\ket{\psi(0)} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(e^{-i\omega_0 t}\ket{+z,-z} - e^{i\omega_0 t}\ket{-z,+z}\right)$$
$$= \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left((\cos\omega_0 t - i\sin\omega_0 t)\ket{+z,-z} - (\cos\omega_0 t + i\sin\omega_0 t)\ket{-z,+z}\right)$$
$$= \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\cos\omega_0 t\left(\ket{+z,-z} - \ket{-z,+z}\right) - i\sin\omega_0 t\left(\ket{+z,-z} + \ket{-z,+z}\right)\right)$$
$$= \cos\omega_0 t\ket{0,0} - i\sin\omega_0 t\ket{1,0}$$
singlet state $\longleftrightarrow$ triplet state
$$T = \frac{\pi}{\omega_0}$$
### Part (c)
**Probabilities of $S_{1x}$ and $S_{2x}$ yielding $\frac{\hbar}{2}$:**
$$P = \left|\bra{+x,+x}\psi(t)\rangle\right|^2$$
$$\ket{+x,+x} = \frac{1}{2}\left(\ket{+z,+z} + \ket{+z,-z} + \ket{-z,+z} + \ket{-z,-z}\right)$$
$$\bra{+x,+x}\psi(t)\rangle = \frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}}\left(\cos\omega_0 t - i\sin\omega_0 t + (-\cos\omega_0 t - i\sin\omega_0 t)\right) = \frac{-i}{\sqrt{2}}\sin\omega_0 t$$
$$P = \left|\bra{+x,+x}\psi(t)\rangle\right|^2 = \frac{1}{2}\sin^2\omega_0 t$$
