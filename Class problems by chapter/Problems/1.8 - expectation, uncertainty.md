The state of a spin-½ particle is given by  
$$|\psi\rangle = \frac{i}{\sqrt{3}} |+z\rangle + \sqrt{\frac{2}{3}} |-z\rangle$$
What are $⟨S_z⟩$ and $\Delta S_z$ for this state?

---
### Solution
Probability weighted average of the eigenstates:
$$\braket{S_z} = \frac{(-i)\cdot i}{3}\frac{\hbar}{2}-\frac{2}{3}\cdot\frac{\hbar}{2} = \left(\frac{1}{3}-\frac{2}{3}\right)\frac{\hbar}{2}=-\frac{\hbar}{6}$$
$$\braket{S_z^2} = \frac{(-i)\cdot i}{3}\frac{\hbar^2}{4}+\frac{2}{3}\cdot\frac{\hbar^2}{4} = \left(\frac{1}{3}+\frac{2}{3}\right)\frac{\hbar^2}{4}=\frac{\hbar^2}{4}$$
$$\Delta S_z =\sqrt{\frac{\hbar^2}{4}-\frac{\hbar^2}{36}}=\sqrt\frac{2\hbar^2}{9}=\frac{\hbar\sqrt2}{3}$$
